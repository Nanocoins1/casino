1. What Is Self-Exclusion?

Self-exclusion is a voluntary process by which a player requests HATHOR Casino to prevent them from accessing their account, placing wagers, or receiving promotional material for a defined period. During the exclusion period, the account is frozen and cannot be reactivated by the player.

3. How to Self-Exclude

Option A — Through Your Account (Fastest)

  1. Log in to your HATHOR account
  2. Go to Settings → Responsible Gambling
  3. Select "Self-Exclusion"
  4. Choose your exclusion type and duration
  5. Confirm the action (a confirmation email will be sent)

Self-exclusion takes effect immediately upon confirmation.

Option B — By Email

Send an email to support@hathor.casino with:

  • Subject line: "Self-Exclusion Request"
  • Your username and registered email address
  • Requested exclusion type and duration

Our support team will process the request within 24 hours and confirm by email.

4. What Happens When You Self-Exclude

  • Account access: You will be unable to log in during the exclusion period
  • Balance: Any remaining balance will be returned to you via the original deposit method (minus applicable fees); KYC may be required
  • Bonuses: All active bonuses are forfeited
  • Marketing: You will be removed from all email, SMS, and push notification marketing lists immediately
  • New accounts: We actively monitor for and close any new accounts you attempt to create
  • Affiliate links: Our affiliate partners are notified and cannot target you with advertising

5. After the Exclusion Period

For cooling-off periods, your account reactivates automatically at the end of the term.

For temporary self-exclusion, reactivation requires:

  1. A written request sent after the exclusion period has fully elapsed
  2. A mandatory 24-hour cooling-off review by our Responsible Gambling team
  3. Confirmation that you have read our Responsible Gambling resources

For permanent self-exclusion, reactivation after 5+ years requires a formal evaluation including independent medical or counselling evidence that continued gambling will not be harmful.

6. Family / Third-Party Requests

If you are a family member concerned about someone's gambling, contact support@hathor.casino. We will require:

  • Proof of relationship or legal authority (e.g., power of attorney, court order)
  • The affected player's name or registered email
  • A statement explaining the concern

We evaluate each request on a case-by-case basis and may temporarily suspend an account pending review.
