1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit them. They allow the website to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and improve your experience. Cookies cannot read data from your hard drive or access other files.

This policy also covers similar technologies we use, including:

  • Local Storage: browser-based storage for preferences and session data
  • Session Storage: temporary data that is cleared when you close the tab
  • Web beacons / pixels: small graphics used to track interaction (primarily in emails)
  • Device fingerprinting: identifying patterns such as screen resolution, timezone, installed fonts (used only for fraud prevention)

2. Categories of Cookies We Use

ESSENTIAL Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be disabled in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions you take such as logging in, filling out forms, or setting preferences. You can block these via your browser, but parts of the site will not work.

FUNCTIONAL Functional Cookies

These enable enhanced functionality and personalization (language preference, theme, game favorites). If disabled, some features will not work properly.

ANALYTICS Analytics Cookies

These help us understand how visitors interact with the site by collecting anonymized information (pages visited, time spent, errors encountered). We use this to improve performance and user experience.

MARKETING Marketing Cookies

These may be set by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests and show relevant advertisements on other sites. They do not store personal information directly but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and device.
