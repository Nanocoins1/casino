Complaint Handling Procedure
Last updated: 2026 · Licensed by Anjouan Online Gaming Commission

HATHOR Casino is committed to resolving all player concerns fairly, transparently, and promptly. This procedure describes how to file a complaint, what timelines apply, and how to escalate if you are not satisfied with our response. This procedure complies with Anjouan Online Gaming Commission licensing requirements.

1. What Qualifies as a Complaint?

A complaint is any expression of dissatisfaction regarding our service, including but not limited to:

  • Game outcomes or perceived technical malfunctions
  • Deposit, withdrawal, or payment processing issues
  • Bonus terms or wagering requirement disputes
  • Account access, KYC, or verification problems
  • Responsible gambling concerns
  • Customer service quality
  • Privacy or data protection matters

2. Before Filing a Formal Complaint

For most issues, our live chat and support email resolve concerns within hours. Try these first — they are often the fastest path to resolution:

  • Live chat: Available 24/7 via the chat icon in the bottom-right corner of the site
  • Email: support@hathor.casino — average first response within 2 hours
  • Self-service: Check Terms, Responsible Gambling, or AML/KYC pages for common answers

3. Formal Complaint Procedure

1
Submit Your Complaint
Send an email to complaints@hathor.casino with the subject line "Formal Complaint". Include: your username, registered email, date of incident, detailed description, supporting evidence (screenshots, transaction IDs, game round IDs), and desired resolution.
2
Acknowledgement
We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours with a unique Complaint Reference Number. Keep this number for all future correspondence.
3
Investigation
Our Compliance Team reviews all evidence, transaction logs, game round data, and any related communications. We may request additional information; please respond promptly to avoid delays.
4
Formal Response
You will receive a written response within 10 business days with our findings, reasoning, and proposed resolution (if any). Complex cases may require up to 30 business days — we will notify you if an extension is needed.
5
Internal Escalation (if unsatisfied)
If you disagree with our decision, reply within 14 days requesting escalation to the Head of Compliance. A senior review will be completed within 10 additional business days.
6
External Dispute Resolution (ADR)
If internal escalation does not resolve the matter, you may refer the complaint to the Anjouan Online Gaming Commission or an approved Alternative Dispute Resolution body. See section 5 below.

If you remain unsatisfied after our internal process has been exhausted, you have the right to escalate externally. HATHOR Casino commits to cooperate fully with any independent review.

6. What We Need From You

To resolve your complaint efficiently, please provide:

  • Your full name as registered on the account
  • Your registered email address
  • Date(s) and time(s) of the incident (UTC preferred)
  • A clear description of what happened and what you expected to happen
  • Any screenshots, transaction IDs, game round IDs, or email correspondence
  • Your desired outcome (refund, account restoration, etc.)

7. Our Commitments

  • Fairness: Every complaint is reviewed on its merits without prejudice
  • Transparency: You will receive clear, reasoned responses — not boilerplate
  • No retaliation: Filing a complaint will never affect your account standing
  • Confidentiality: Complaint details are shared only with personnel directly involved in resolution and with regulators when legally required
  • Record-keeping: All complaints are logged and retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by our license

HATHOR Casino publishes anonymized complaint statistics annually as part of our license transparency obligations. These will be made available on request to compliance@hathor.casino.
