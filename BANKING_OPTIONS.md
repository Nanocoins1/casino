## 📋 Application Strategy

### What to submit (same package for all):
1. **UK LTD certificate of incorporation** (Companies House)
2. **Anjouan license** (provisional or issued)
3. **Directors/UBO documents:**
   - Passports
   - Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
4. **Business plan** — casino operations, target markets, projected volumes
5. **AML/KYC Policy** ✅ *(We have this — /aml.html on our site)*
6. **Responsible Gambling Policy** ✅ *(Already live)*
7. **Complaints Handling Procedure** ✅ *(Already live)*
8. **Self-Exclusion Procedure** ✅ *(Already live)*
9. **Terms & Conditions** ✅ *(Already live)*
10. **Expected transaction volumes** (monthly deposits/withdrawals estimates)
11. **Source of funds** — initial capital origin
12. **Website URL** (they will check your compliance pages)

### Language to use in applications:
- ✅ "Licensed online gaming operator"
- ✅ "Regulated entertainment platform"
- ✅ "iGaming B2C"
- ✅ "Anjouan Online Gaming Commission licensed"
- ❌ Avoid: "casino" alone, "gambling" alone, "bet"

### Timeline realistic:
- **Tier 1 banks:** 2-4 weeks
- **Tier 2 EMIs:** 1-3 weeks
- **Tier 3 offshore:** 4-8 weeks (more paperwork)

### Parallel strategy:
**Apply to 3-4 banks simultaneously.** Do not wait for one rejection before applying to the next.

**Recommended parallel applications:**
1. Bank Frick (primary — highest reliability)
2. BCB Group (UK base, good match)
3. Cashaa (fast onboarding)
4. Clear Junction (backup)

Whichever approves first wins. Others can remain as backup accounts (never rely on a single banking relationship in iGaming).

## 🚨 Red Flags to Watch

**If a bank/broker asks for:**
- ⚠️ Large upfront "activation fee" (>€3,000 before verification) — SCAM
- ⚠️ Deposits into personal accounts of employees — SCAM
- ⚠️ Crypto-only payment before services rendered — SCAM

Legitimate banks charge via invoices once account is approved. Always verify via official websites, not links from emails.
